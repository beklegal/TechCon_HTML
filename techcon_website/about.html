History

TechCon began in 2010 with the mission to bring together the brightest minds in technology. From its humble beginnings, it has grown into a global event featuring thousands of attendees and renowned speakers.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation, facilitate learning, and foster connections among tech enthusiasts and professionals. We strive to provide an inclusive and forward-thinking platform for ideas that shape the future.

Past Speakers

  • Ada Lovelace – Visionary in computing theory and algorithmic design.
  • Alan Turing – Pioneer of modern computing and artificial intelligence.
  • Grace Hopper – Inventor of the compiler and advocate for programming languages.
